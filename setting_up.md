##setting up dual monitors system-wide with xrandr on debian
>VGA1 \- external monitor
>LVDS1 \- internal monitor

`xrandr --output VGA1 --mode 1920x1080 --pos 1366x0 --output LVDS1 --mode 1366x768 --pos 0x312`
